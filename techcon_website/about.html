Our History

Since its launch in 2010, TechCon has grown from a local developer meetup into a world-renowned technology conference that attracts thousands of attendees from across the globe. With each year, we've expanded our scope, welcoming cutting-edge innovations and thought leaders in AI, cybersecurity, software engineering, and beyond.

Our Mission

TechCon exists to empower people through technology. Our mission is to connect innovators, foster collaboration, and promote responsible tech development that benefits society. We believe in a future driven by inclusive, sustainable, and ethical innovation.

Past Speakers

Dr. Maya Chen

A leading researcher in quantum computing, Dr. Chen has pioneered methods to scale quantum algorithms for enterprise use. She delivered a groundbreaking talk in 2022 on democratizing access to quantum platforms.

Raj Patel

Founder of GreenCircuit AI, Raj spoke at TechCon 2021 about the intersection of AI and sustainability. His work in optimizing smart grids has reshaped how cities approach energy efficiency.

Lina Torres

A cybersecurity strategist, Lina delivered a compelling keynote in 2023 on zero-trust security models. Her open-source toolkit has been adopted by organizations globally.
